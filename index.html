Pitch

Defend yourself from a horde of huge eye monsters by building your very own fighting robot - Kill-BOI 9000, your new best friend and toaster. Choose from a large variety of weapons and combine them to make them even larger and ridiculously powerful! Like shooting burning cannon balls.

Description

- Upgrade Kill-BOI -
Supply Kill-BOI 9000 with your enemies' hearts, so he can gain power.

- Find Weapon Synergies -
Drones that have circling saws? Sure!
Grenades that contain Black Holes? Of course!
Burning cannonballs? Hell yeah!

- Gamble For Loot -
Pull on Kill-BOI's lever to win awesome gear for your human!

- Play With a Friend -
Fight with a friend in local Co-op (requires a friend).

Features

  • Create a unique build every run with different combinations of weapons, upgrades, and lots of weapon crossovers.
  • Survive for 10 minutes, then enter endless mode and try to come farther than any other human in the world!
  • Equip your human with hats, body armor and weapons with unique effects
  • Explore the Wasteland, the Jungle and the Moon
  • Sustain through challenging weather events
  • Master the game to survive Hard Mode!
  • Collect achievements!
